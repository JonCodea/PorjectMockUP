Book Sustainability

Silent Spring

Rachel Carson · 1962

The book that started modern environmentalism, by documenting what pesticides were doing to the living world.

Why it's here

The founding text for the idea that an environmental harm can be evidenced, named and stopped.
Book Sustainability

Doughnut Economics

Kate Raworth · 2017

Reframes economics around a doughnut: a social floor nobody should fall below, an ecological ceiling nobody should exceed.

Why it's here

Gives students a picture of sustainability that is about justice, not only carbon.
Book Sustainability

This Changes Everything

Naomi Klein · 2014

Argues the climate crisis is not a failure of individual behaviour but of an economic model.

Why it's here

Connects the Sustainability and Neoliberalism themes directly to one another.
Book Sustainability

The Uninhabitable Earth

David Wallace-Wells · 2019

An unflinching account of what several degrees of warming would actually mean.

Why it's here

Useful precisely because it is hard to read - it makes abstraction concrete.
